UHS

UNIT HANDLING SOLUTIONS

Simple and Easy customised conveyor system for the sewing line which helps to reduce the man power, rework, WIP and keeps the area clean with visibility of the line, smooth flow of cut pieces and achievement of planned output with case.

DDBM

DIGITAL DATA BOARDS MANAGEMENT

Process based android real time quality management software for solving quality issues in the present and future. Also to get the real time production data anytime anywhere.

VAS

VISUAL ANALYSIS SYSTEMA

VAS is a IE tool for the industrial engineers for making effective OB charts, line balancing, SMV and skill matrix management the OB chart is very important too used in garment costing, production planning and actual production.

FAMS

FIXED ASSET MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Machine tracking, stock status, Assets planning, Allocation and maintenance software can locate the machines leading to better utilization of the machines and spares.

DDBM QM

It contains the input and report pages for rejection pieces and defect pieces. Also gives the size wise defect report & consolidated report of defect pieces. Moreover, day wise hourly report for defect is also available.

DDBM MIS

It gives the detailed report about the stitching, despatch, WIP and OCR reports.

DDBM WIP

It gives the overall / consolidated section wisreport on WIP from cutting to packing, i.e., from starting till ending.
