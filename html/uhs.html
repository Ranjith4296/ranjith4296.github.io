WHAT IS UHS

UHS - UNIT HANDLING SOLUTIONS is a simple and easy customized conveyor system for the sewing finishing line as well as movement between inter departments. It reduces Manpower, Handling time, Rework, WIP and Clear visibility of the Line. Smooth flow of cut pieces and achievement of the planned output with ease. Our existing customers are reporting production improvement of over 20% over bundle system.
